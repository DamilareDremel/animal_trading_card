Dog

A dog's sense of smell is estimated to be 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than that of humans.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 60 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: Human environments

With a diverse array of breeds, dogs vary greatly in size, appearance, and temperament, making them suitable for a wide range of roles. From loyal family pets to skilled working partners, dogs have adapted to various environments and tasks.
